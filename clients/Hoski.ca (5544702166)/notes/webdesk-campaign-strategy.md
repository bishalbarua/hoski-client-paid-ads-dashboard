## Business Summary

Hoski's Web Desk service sells a results-first outcome: a website that actively converts visitors into customers through speed optimization, conversion-focused design, and tracking setup. Primary CTA is "Book a Strategy Call." Target buyer: SMB owner or marketing manager frustrated with an underperforming website who wants an agency that can prove ROI.

**Value prop:** "Turn Your Website Into a 24/7 Sales Machine"
**Estimated ATV:** $5,000–$25,000+ CAD per project
**Purchase cycle:** 2–6 weeks (considered purchase)
**Geography:** Canada — priority on Montreal, Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary

## 30-60-90 Day Roadmap

**Days 1–30:** Launch Tier 1 (3 campaigns). Upload shared negative list on day 1. Set baseline KPI targets: CPC $4–$12, CTR 4%+, CPL under $150 CAD. Conversion tracking confirmed firing.

**Days 31–60:** Pull search term reports weekly, add negatives, pause underperforming assets. Launch Tier 2 (ecommerce + industry verticals + competitor). Begin building remarketing audience (300+ users needed).

**Days 61–90:** Switch winning campaigns to Maximize Conversions (30+ conversions required). Activate remarketing display. Test Performance Max if conversion volume supports.
