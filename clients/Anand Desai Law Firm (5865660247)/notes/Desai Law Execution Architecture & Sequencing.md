# **First Slide**

# **Desai Law Execution Architecture & Sequencing**

## **90 Day Implementation Pipeline Objective**

Establish a fully measurable, conversion-ready website and generate consistent qualified leads within the first 60 days, while building a strong long-term SEO foundation.

# **Slide 2**

# **Phase 1 : Tracking & Infrastructure Setup**

**Timeline: Week 1**

### **What we’re doing**

We are building the measurement layer that allows us to track every interaction and optimize performance based on real data.

### **Key implementations**

* CallRail setup (call tracking \+ attribution)  
* Google Analytics 4 (GA4)  
* Google Tag Manager (GTM)  
* Event tracking:  
  * Phone calls  
  * Form submissions  
* Source attribution (Google Ads, Organic, Direct, etc.)

### **Outcome**

* Every call and lead is tracked and attributed  
* We establish a clean baseline for performance

**Timeline: Weeks 1–2**

### **What we’re doing**

Improving the website’s ability to convert visitors into calls and inquiries.
