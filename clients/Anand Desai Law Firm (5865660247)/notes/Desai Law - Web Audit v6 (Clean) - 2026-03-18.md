### ---

**What This Means**

Desai is bringing a 2-page brochure to a 20-page content war. Both competitors have built page systems that capture specific, high-intent searches (“truck accident lawyer Glendale,” “pedestrian accident attorney Glendale”). Desai has one page trying to rank for all of them.

That is not a strategy problem. It is a structural ceiling.

### ---

**Revenue Bridge**

* **Zero analytics \= zero attribution.** If the firm spends anything on ads, SEO, or referral development, there is no way to know what works. Installing GA4 with conversion tracking typically reveals that 40-60% of spend can be reallocated for better results.  
* **Missing service pages \= invisible for high-intent queries.** A Glendale PI firm without a dedicated “car accident lawyer Glendale” page cannot rank for that search. That single query type generates dozens of monthly searches with high purchase intent. Every missing page is a missed ranking slot against firms that have built those pages.  
* **Review volume gap \= local pack disadvantage.** Google’s local pack algorithm weights review count and recency heavily. **27 reviews competing against 110 and 191** creates a structural disadvantage in the 3-pack, where the majority of local legal clicks happen.

### ---

**The Invisible Leak**

**Broken stat counters are actively communicating the opposite of reality.**

The homepage has two prominent counter elements meant to showcase “15+ Years Experience” and “100+ Jury Trials Conducted.” Both display “0+”. This is likely a JavaScript counter animation that fails to trigger on page load.

A visitor who sees “0+ Years Experience” and “0+ Jury Trials” receives the opposite signal from what was intended. The firm’s single strongest differentiator (trial experience that most PI attorneys cannot match) is being undermined by a broken widget.

Without analytics installed, this bug has no detection mechanism and may have been live for weeks or months.

---

**Second invisible leak: No tracking \= no retargeting.** Without any pixel installed, every visitor who leaves without calling is lost permanently. For high-ticket legal services where prospects visit 3-5 sites before deciding, this means the firm cannot stay visible to warm prospects.
