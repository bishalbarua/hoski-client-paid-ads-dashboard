**Reality Check (E-com)**

Let’s keep this clean.

* This is not a traffic problem. It’s a **conversion \+ intent problem**.  
* We’re paying for clicks without forcing decisions.  
* Product pages look good, but they don’t **sell**.  
* Ads are either:  
  * Too broad → bad traffic  
  * Too soft → no purchase intent  
* Feed is under-leveraged → Shopping isn’t doing its job

**What that creates:**

* Low CVR  
* Unstable CPA  
* Slow learning

We don’t need more traffic.  
We need **better traffic \+ more pressure \+ faster feedback**.

## **2\. Starting Point Strategy (Move Fast Model)**

We’re not doing phases.  
We run everything at once:

**Conversion \+ Acquisition \+ Signal**

---

### **A. Conversion (Fix while traffic is live)**

No redesign. Just make it sell.

**Product Page — first 2 scrolls only:**

* Clear value: what it is \+ why it matters  
* Price \+ incentive visible  
* Strong CTA

**Add fast:**

* 3–5 real reviews  
* 1 UGC video (non-negotiable)  
* Bullet points:  
  * Materials  
  * Sustainability angle  
  * Durability

**Lower section:**

* Simple comparison (why this vs others)  
* Shipping / returns / warranty

**Boosters:**

* Free shipping or simple offer  
* Light urgency (don’t overdo it)

---

### **B. Acquisition (Revenue starts immediately)**

#### **Google (primary driver)**

**1\. Shopping**

* Only best products  
* Fix titles:  
  * keyword \+ benefit (not just name)

**2\. Search (high intent only)**

* “buy sustainable watch”  
* “eco friendly watches”  
* Exact \+ Phrase only

**3\. Brand**

* Cheap wins, protect demand

---

#### **Meta (don’t overcomplicate it)**

Run 3 angles:

1. Gift → *“a gift that actually means something”*  
2. Identity → *“wear what you stand for”*  
3. Aesthetic → clean lifestyle

**Rules:**

* UGC \> polished  
* Hook in first 3 seconds or it dies

---

#### **Retargeting (after day 5\)**

* Visitors \+ add-to-cart  
* Simple reminder \+ small push

---

### **C. Signal (how we make decisions)**

Tracking doesn’t need to be perfect, just usable.

Track:

* View  
* Add to cart  
* Checkout  
* Purchase

---

### **Daily filter (non-negotiable)**

* Spending, no conversions → cut or fix  
* Converting → scale  
* Getting clicks but no sales → page or offer problem

No debates. Test and move.

---

### **Timeline (first 14 days)**

**Days 1–3**

* Patch product page

**Days 4–7**

* Cut bad traffic

## **Overall Reality Check**

* The account is not underperforming because of the product. It’s underperforming because the system around it is incomplete.  
* Right now there is no reliable loop between **traffic → data → decisions → optimization**.  
* SEO is weak → low organic discovery, no authority, no compounding growth.  
* Ads are likely either:  
  * Too broad (wasting budget), or  
  * Too cautious (not generating enough data).  
* The website looks clean but does not push decisions:  
  * Low urgency  
  * Weak differentiation  
  * Limited proof  
* Tracking is either incomplete or underutilized → which means decisions are being made partially blind.

**Net effect:**

* Money goes out, but insights don’t come back fast enough.  
* Optimization cycles are too slow.  
* Growth is reactive, not engineered.

**Important:**  
We are not fixing a campaign.  
We are building a system that produces predictable outcomes.

## **2\. Proposed Starting Point Strategy (Parallel Execution Model)**

We are not working in phases.  
We are running **Foundation \+ Cleaning \+ Revenue Engine at the same time**.

---

### **A. Foundation (Good Enough to Launch)**

**Objective:** Enable data collection without slowing execution.

* Tracking:  
  * GA4 \+ GTM verified  
  * Events:  
    * View Content  
    * Add to Cart  
    * Initiate Checkout  
    * Purchase  
* Platform sync:  
  * Google Ads \+ Meta tracking aligned  
* Offer clarity:  
  * Define 1 core angle (gift / identity / aesthetic)  
  * Add 1 incentive (free shipping OR discount)  
* Landing page (minimum viable fix):  
  * Clear value proposition above the fold  
  * Single primary CTA  
  * Add:  
    * 3–5 reviews  
    * Basic trust signals  
  * Remove obvious friction (clutter, slow elements)

**Standard:**  
If it works at \~70%, we launch.

---

### **B. Cleaning (While Live)**

**Objective:** Reduce waste and improve signal quality without stopping traffic.

* Google Ads:  
  * Remove irrelevant search terms  
  * Add negative keywords daily (first 7–10 days)  
  * Separate:  
    * Brand  
    * Non-brand  
* Meta Ads:  
  * Pause low CTR / low engagement creatives  
  * Keep early winners (even if not fully optimized)  
* Website:  
  * Improve only:  
    * First scroll  
    * Product page clarity  
  * Add:  
    * 1 UGC video  
    * 1 comparison or proof section

---

### **C. Revenue Engine (Launch Immediately)**

**Objective:** Generate revenue \+ data from day 1\.

#### **Google Ads**

* Shopping Campaign (priority)  
  * Optimized product titles (keyword \+ benefit)  
  * Focus on best products only  
* Search Campaign (high intent only)  
  * Exact / Phrase:  
    * “buy sustainable watch”  
    * “eco friendly watches”  
  * No broad testing initially  
* Brand Campaign  
  * Capture existing demand efficiently

---

#### **Meta Ads**

Launch only 3 angles:

1. Gift:  
   * “A gift that actually means something”  
2. Identity:  
   * “What you wear says what you stand for”  
3. Aesthetic:  
   * Clean, minimal lifestyle

**Creative rule:**

* Hook in first 3 seconds  
* UGC \> polished content

---

#### **Retargeting (Day 5–7)**

* Audiences:  
  * Site visitors  
  * Add-to-cart users  
* Messaging:  
  * Reminder \+ simple incentive

---

### **D. Execution Timeline (First 14 Days)**

**Days 1–3**

* Tracking setup  
* Launch:  
  * Google Shopping  
  * Search (high intent)  
  * Meta campaigns  
* Apply minimum landing page fixes

**Days 4–7**

* Cut wasted spend  
* Add negatives  
* Replace weak creatives  
* Add UGC to product pages

**Days 8–14**

* Increase budget on early winners  
* Start retargeting  
* Apply small CRO improvements

---

### **E. Decision Framework (Daily Use)**

* What is spending and not converting?  
  → Cut or fix immediately  
* What is converting?  
  → Scale budget  
* What is unclear?  
  → Test quickly (do not debate)

---
