# Competitor Research — Custom Furniture
**Client:** Park Road Custom Furniture and Decor
**Date:** 2026-03-18
**Context:** Research to evaluate offer strategy for custom furniture campaign ($750 off $5k+)

---

## Park Road Current Positioning

**Website:** parkroadfurniture.com
**Location:** 761 Fanshawe Park Road, London, Ontario (founded 2012, formerly Warehouse 74)
**What They Sell:** Built-to-order custom furniture — living room, dining room, bedroom. Solid wood, steel, pipe, glass. Styles: Modern, Modern Country, Traditional, Farmhouse/Cottage.
**Current Positioning:** Canadian-made, sustainability-focused, craftsmanship-led. "Buy local, solid construction, long-term value over mass-market."
**Current Offers:** None. No pricing, promotions, or discounts publicly displayed.
**CTA:** Browse categories → contact for custom quote. Inquiry-driven, not e-commerce.

## Competitor Profiles

### 1. This Oak House
**URL:** thisoakhouse.ca

**What They Sell:** Amish and Mennonite handcrafted furniture — all room categories
**Positioning:** Artisan heritage and craftsmanship provenance (Amish/Mennonite origin)

**CTA:** Product browsing → contact for custom inquiries

### 2. Mennonite Crafted Furniture
**URL:** mennonitecraftedfurniture.com
**Location:** London, Ontario (direct local competitor)
**What They Sell:** Solid wood handcrafted furniture — all categories, reclaimed wood, Fjords chairs
**Positioning:** "Family owned since 1977 — one of the oldest Mennonite furniture stores in Canada." Core message: solid wood, handcrafted, built to last a lifetime.
**Active Promotions:**
- *"LOCAL DELIVERY AND COMPLETE SET UP IN YOUR HOME — ONLY $20.00 FOR ENTIRE MONTH OF MARCH 2026"*
- Category sale on dining room and select leather furniture
**CTA:** Specials page maintained year-round
**Key Takeaway:** Only local competitor running an active promotion. Uses objection removal (delivery cost) rather than % discount. Specials page is a permanent fixture.

### 3. Rustix Studio
**URL:** rustixstudio.ca
**Location:** St. Thomas / London area, Ontario
**What They Sell:** Custom handcrafted tables from sustainably sourced local hardwoods, live-edge pieces, residential and commercial
**Positioning:** *"#ORDERSOMETHINGRUSTIX — WE MAKE COOL SH*T."* Modern luxury furniture with fine craftsmanship. Portfolio-forward.
**Active Promotions:** None. No pricing or discounts displayed.
**CTA:** Gallery, workshop events, email list signup
**Key Takeaway:** Pure craft and aesthetic positioning. Community/email list building rather than price incentives.

---

### 4. Naked Furniture
**URL:** nakedfurniture.ca
**Location:** Etobicoke (Toronto), ON — 12,000 sq ft showroom, ships Canada-wide
**What They Sell:** Solid wood furniture all categories, custom staining, refinishing. "Largest solid wood and unpainted furniture store in Canada since 1962."
**Positioning:** *"Just Name It, We Make It!"* — customization at scale
**Active Promotions:**
- *"Early Holiday Offer: Enjoy 15% off + an additional 10% if you order before the New Year!"*
- Sale items showing 35–37% off (e.g., Duke Side Chair: -37%, from $3,660 → $2,310 CAD)
**Key Takeaway:** Most discount-aggressive competitor. Uses stacked % offers and seasonal urgency. Sets the discount benchmark for the national market.

---

### 5. Acre Made
**URL:** shopacremade.com
**Location:** Ontario — white-glove delivery GTA + Southwestern Ontario, ships Canada + US
**What They Sell:** Canadian-made custom dining tables, coffee tables, console tables. Both custom and ready-to-ship.
**Positioning:** Premium artisan brand. Collaboration with designer Louie Hewitt. *"One-of-a-kind pieces that will last for years to come."*
**Pricing:** Tables start $5,990–$6,950 CAD
**Active Promotions:**
- *"Get $500 off your first order of $5,000 or more! Sign up below to claim your discount."* — email capture gated offer
**Key Takeaway:** Most strategically sophisticated offer mechanic. Dollar amount + threshold + email capture. Directly comparable to Park Road's target market.

---

### 6. Woodcraft
**URL:** woodcraft.ca
**Location:** Toronto + Canada-wide
**What They Sell:** Handmade solid wood furniture — all categories. *"Custom, Canadian Since 1974."*
**Positioning:** Heritage Canadian manufacturer, quality at accessible price points
**Active Promotions:**
- *"March Madness – 30% Storewide | Sale Ends March 31st"*
- "Biggest Floor Model Sale" ongoing
**Key Takeaway:** Named seasonal sales with hard end dates create urgency. 30% storewide is an aggressive, easy-to-communicate offer.

## Gap Analysis

### 🔴 Critical Gap — Park Road Has No Promotion
Every national competitor uses some promotional mechanism. 3 of 6 competitors use active discounts. Park Road currently has nothing to incentivize first contact.

### 🟢 White Space — Free Consultation CTA
Zero competitors offer a free design consultation as a conversion CTA. For a $5k+ purchase this removes significant friction and qualifies leads before they visit the showroom.

### 🟢 White Space — Dollar Amount Offer
Only Acre Made uses a flat dollar-amount offer ($500 off $5k+). This format is more tangible than percentage discounts and better suited to high-ticket custom furniture.

---

## Offer Recommendation

**Original proposal:** 15% off orders $10,000+
**Problem:** $10k threshold screens out most buyers. Average custom order likely $3k–$8k.

**Recommended offer:** *"$750 off your first custom order of $5,000 or more"*

Why:
- Dollar amounts feel more tangible than percentages
- $5k threshold qualifies real buyers without being out of reach
- "First order" creates urgency to act now
- Can be gated behind email/form signup (Acre Made model)
- Ad headline: `$750 Off Custom Furniture — Orders $5,000+`

**Secondary tier (optional):** "$1,500 off orders over $10,000 — March only" for larger projects
