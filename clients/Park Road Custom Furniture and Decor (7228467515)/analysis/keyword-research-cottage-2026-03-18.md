# Keyword Research — Cottage Furniture Campaign
**Client:** Park Road Custom Furniture and Decor
**Date:** 2026-03-18
**Campaign:** Cottage Furniture — Lead Generation
**Geo:** Ontario + Canada (cottage buyers are province-wide, not just London)
**Goal:** Form submissions + phone calls
**Match Type Strategy:** Exact + Phrase for launch. No broad match until 30+ conversions.
**Data Source:** Google Ads Keyword Planner API — Ontario (20119) + Canada (2124), two seed batches merged

---

## Key Intelligence from the Data

### 1. Muskoka is a Massive Search Signal — 8,100/mo
"Muskoka chairs" and variations pull 8,100 searches/month in Ontario. "Muskoka furniture" pulls 880/mo.
This confirms the competitor research finding: **Muskoka is the highest-value geographic identity signal in Ontario cottage culture.** No competitor is specifically bidding on it for custom furniture. Park Road should own this term.

### 2. A Named Competitor Has Brand Search Volume — Simply Cottage
"Simply Cottage Bracebridge" = 480/mo. "Simply cottage furniture" = 40/mo. "Pioneer Furniture Muskoka" = 30/mo.
These are real brands with real search demand. Add them as negatives to avoid wasted impressions, and consider a separate competitor campaign if budget allows.

### 3. Farmhouse Dining Table is the Highest-Volume Product Category
"Farm house dining table" and variations = **880/mo each** across multiple query forms.
This is the single biggest keyword cluster in the cottage/rustic furniture space. High volume, high intent, affordable CPCs ($0.54–$1.95). Build this ad group first.

### 4. CPCs Are Cheap Across the Board
Range: $0.32–$4.38. "Cottage dining table" has the highest high bid at $4.38 — still cheap for a $5k+ order.
At $1.50 avg CPC and 400 monthly clicks, that's **$600/month to own the cottage furniture search market in Ontario**.

### 5. Geo Note — Ontario-Wide, Not Just London
Cottage buyers are in Toronto, Barrie, Kingston, Ottawa — driving to or ordering for cottages in Muskoka, Georgian Bay, Haliburton, Prince Edward County. Park Road delivers regionally. **Target all of Ontario, not just London.**

## Volume & Budget Reality Check

**Estimated Ontario-wide volume across all ad groups: ~8,000–12,000/mo**

This is substantially larger than the London-only custom furniture campaign (~4,500/mo).
Farmhouse dining table terms alone contribute 3,000–4,000 impressions/month.

| Scenario | Clicks/mo | Spend/mo | Leads (5% CVR) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative ($500/mo) | 300–350 | $500 | 15–18 |
| Moderate ($800/mo) | 500–550 | $800 | 25–28 |
| Full market ($1,200/mo) | 750–850 | $1,200 | 37–43 |

At a $5,000+ average order value, **1 closed sale per month covers 6–12 months of ad spend.**

## How This Campaign Differs from the Custom Furniture Campaign

| | Custom Furniture Campaign | Cottage Furniture Campaign |
|---|---|---|
| **Geo** | London, Ontario primary | Ontario-wide |
| **Buyer** | Anyone wanting custom furniture | Cottage owner / farmhouse aesthetic buyer |
| **Volume** | ~4,500/mo total | ~8,000–12,000/mo total |
| **Messaging** | Custom, built-to-order, showroom | Muskoka lifestyle, cottage durability, heirloom quality |
| **Top keyword** | furniture store london ontario (1,900) | farmhouse dining table variations (880 each) |
| **Landing page** | Same offer page ($750 off $5k+) | Same or separate page with cottage imagery |

**Recommendation:** These can share the same landing page and offer but use different ad copy. The cottage campaign should feature cottage/Muskoka imagery and language. The custom campaign should lead with the showroom and built-to-order angle.
