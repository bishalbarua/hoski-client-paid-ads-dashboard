## Critical Geo Note

London, Ontario is a small market. Custom furniture terms pull **10–30 searches/month** locally.
"Custom furniture london ontario" = 30/mo. "Custom made furniture" = 10/mo.

**Recommendation:** Expand geo targeting to Southwestern Ontario to build sufficient volume.
Include: London, Kitchener-Waterloo, Guelph, Windsor, Sarnia, Woodstock, St. Thomas.
Park Road delivers regionally — this is justified and won't waste budget.

## Volume Reality Check

This is a small local market. Total addressable search volume across all ad groups = ~4,500/mo.
At an average CTR of 8–12% on search, expect **360–540 clicks/month** at full coverage.
At $1.50 average CPC, that's ~**$540–$810/month in spend** to capture the market.

This means:
- Budget $500–$800/month captures most of the relevant search market in London
- If CPL target is $50–$80 per lead, you need 6–10 leads/month to hit budget
- At 5–10% form conversion rate on landing page, that's realistic from 540 clicks

**To increase volume without wasting spend:**
1. Expand geo to Southwestern Ontario (Kitchener, Waterloo, Windsor, Guelph)
2. Add display/retargeting to reach people who visited but didn't convert
3. After 60 days, use search term data to find additional long-tail terms
