## GOALS

| Goal | Target |
|---|---|

Work with Christopher to pull these segments from GHL or Lightspeed POS:

## GHL TECHNICAL SETUP

### Campaign Architecture in GHL

Build as two separate email campaigns (not workflows, unless re-engagement automation is desired):

| GHL Element | Track 1 | Track 2 |
|---|---|---|
| Campaign name | `April 2026 — Custom Furniture Offer` | `April 2026 — Cottage Furniture Offer` |
| List/Tag | `Custom Furniture — April 2026` | `Cottage Furniture — April 2026` |
| From name | `Chris at Park Road Furniture` | `Chris at Park Road Furniture` |
| From email | Confirm with Christopher — use verified GHL sending domain |
| Reply-to | Christopher's actual email (so replies go to him) |

### Re-engagement Automation (Email 4)

Set up a simple GHL automation:
- Trigger: Email 1 or Email 2 opened
- Condition: No link clicked within 5 days of open
- Action: Send Email 4 (re-engagement version for respective track)

### Link Tracking

All links in both tracks must use GHL tracked links so you can see click-through rates per email.

**UTM parameters to add to all landing page URLs:**

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| utm_source | email |
| utm_medium | email |
| utm_campaign | april-custom-750 (Track 1) / april-cottage-750 (Track 2) |
| utm_content | email1 / email2 / email3 / email4 |

Example tracked URL for Track 1, Email 1:
`https://go.parkroadfurniture.com/custom-furniture?utm_source=email&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=april-custom-750&utm_content=email1`

This lets you see in Google Analytics (or GHL reporting) which email drove form fills on the landing page.

### Unsubscribe Compliance

- GHL will add an unsubscribe footer automatically — confirm it's working before first send
- Remove unsubscribers from all future Park Road email lists (one-click unsubscribe required under CASL)
- Do not email anyone who previously unsubscribed from the GHL list

Track these per campaign after each email send:

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Open rate | 25%+ (warm list, known brand) |
