## Campaign Setup Notes

### Ad Naming Convention (Batch 7)
```
[Angle Name] V[#] — [Variation Letter]

Examples:
- Price Shock V1 - A
- Authority V1 - B
- Transformation V1 - C
- Affordability Proof V1 - A
```

### Recommended A/B Testing Structure
- Launch 1 primary text variation per angle (4 ads minimum)
- Test the strongest headline from each angle
- After 7–14 days, identify winner by CPL and lead quality
- Pause underperformers; scale winner with additional variations

### Key Rules (Do Not Break)
1. ✅ "New smile in 24 hours" / "same-day new smile" = CORRECT
2. ❌ "Permanent teeth in 24 hours" / "Zirconium in 24 hours" = NEVER USE
3. Always include Free Consultation + Free CT Scan offer
4. Pricing anchor: **~$23,920 / "under $24,000"** (20% off $29,900) — always reference the discount
5. Always contrast with ClearChoice in price angles (Dr. Voit was their Clinical Director)
6. Link to **go.voitdental.com/new-discount-offer** (NOT the old /dental-implants LP)
